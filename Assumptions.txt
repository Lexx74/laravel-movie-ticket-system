List of Assumptions for the final CISC332 Project
Team 118 - Quentin Petraroia, Luis Rivera-Wong, Braedan Robinson

1. There will be ONE address per user/theatre complex/supplier 

2. There will be ONE phone number per user/theatre complex/supplier

3. Only a registered user can leave a movie review 

4. We assume that movie plot synopsis and movie reviews will be longer than 255 characters; hence the TEXT data type.
